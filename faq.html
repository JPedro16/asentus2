General Questions

Our company is headquartered in the Netherlands.

We work using a proven agile system. Projects are divided into weekly sprints and smaller, clearly defined tasks that are completed step by step. All tasks are continuously tracked, prioritized, and quality-checked. To keep you informed and address questions quickly, we hold regular weekly follow-up meetings.

There are two options. We can use our own infrastructure, including our systems, licenses, and cloud environment, where all data is available to you at any time for download. Alternatively, we can work directly on your company's systems using secure remote access software.

A non-disclosure agreement (NDA) is standard in all our collaborations. We treat all your data and information with strict confidentiality. Our team members are contractually bound to confidentiality, ensuring your business secrets are reliably protected.

Getting started is simple. During the initial online meeting, we agree on a start date together. No upfront payment is required, and the first 150 hours serve as a non-binding trial period. You will only receive a monthly invoice once you are fully satisfied with our services. We work with you for as long as you need us—without any long-term contractual commitment.
