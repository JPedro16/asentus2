Privacy Policy

Tech Mechancix B.V. values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This page explains what data we process, why we process it, and what rights you have under the General Data Protection Regulation.

General

Tech Mechancix B.V. values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. We collect personal data about you when you use our website or services, or when you provide this information to us.

As a data controller under the General Data Protection Regulation, Tech Mechancix B.V. is fully compliant with all applicable laws and regulations governing the processing of personal data.

Our priority is to process the personal data you provide to us with utmost care. This privacy statement provides a clear overview of the personal data we collect, how we handle it, and the reasons why we collect it.

A Summary Of The Personal Data We Process

A. From you as a user of our website or services

  1. IP address
  2. Phone number
  3. Email address
  4. Type of browser and device
  5. Other personal data that you actively provide
  6. Data about your activities on our website or using our services
  7. Location
  8. First and last name

Sensitive Personal Data We Process

A. Minors

  1. Our website and services do not intend to collect data about visitors who are under the age of 16, except with the permission of parents or legal guardians. Tech Mechancix B.V. cannot verify the age of visitors, so we encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities to prevent the collection of their personal data without proper consent.

B. Please contact us if we have made a mistake

  1. If you believe that we have collected personal data about a minor without proper consent, please contact us immediately at hr@TechMechancix.nl. We take these matters seriously and will promptly delete any data found to be in violation of our policies.

Why We Need Your Data And For How Long

A. Tech Mechancix B.V. processes your personal data with the following purposes:

We take the protection of your personal data seriously and will only retain it for as long as necessary to fulfill the above purposes or as required by law.

  1. To send you requested information as a result of filling out the contact form; and
  2. To analyze your behavior on the website to improve our website and tailor it to your preferences.
  3. To be able to e-mail you when this is necessary to provide our services;

B. The duration for which we store your data

  1. Tech Mechancix B.V. will not retain your personal data for a period longer than is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which the data is collected. If you would like to know the retention period in your specific case, please contact: hr@TechMechancix.nl

Sharing With Others

  1. With the exception of the cookies we use, as outlined in our cookie policy, any personal data collected through this website will not be shared with third parties.

Mapping Out Your Visit Of Our Website

A. Cookies

  1. A cookie is a small text file that is stored by the browser of your computer, tablet or smartphone when you first visit this website.
  2. Tech Mechancix B.V. uses different types of cookies:
    1. Tracking cookies: These cookies are placed by an external party. By reading these cookies, the advertiser recognizes you when you visit a site in which they are also involved. If you continue browsing, personalized ads can be shown. These cookies can also keep track of your online behaviour, which may result in a very specific profile being built by these parties. This profile can be so detailed that it is considered personal data.
    2. Functional cookies: These are cookies with a purely technical functionality. These ensure that the website works properly, so that it can, for example, remember your preferred settings. Using these, we can also optimize our website and the services through our website.
    3. Analytic cookies by third parties: These cookies can keep track of your online behaviour. These cookies are placed when using analytical service providers, such as Google Analytics.

B. Cookie notification and what to do when you don't like cookies

  1. Your cookie preferences were set by you on your initial visit to our website. Non-essential cookies can be denied on a per-category basis. Placement of cookies, other than functional or anonymous analytical cookies, will only occur after an active action by you as a visitor. You have the ability to change your preferences at any time through our website.

Accessing, Adjusting Or Deleting Your Data

A. Your rights

  1. You have the right to
    1. request that less of your data be processed by us,
    2. apply for the removal of your data,
    3. access your personal data,
    4. request an adjustment of your data
    5. have your data transferred to someone else and
    6. file a complaint as described in article 8.
  2. If you have any questions or comments about the way Tech Mechancix B.V. processes your data, please send them to hr@TechMechancix.nl
  3. Tech Mechancix B.V. wants to be sure that the request has been made by you. That is why we kindly ask that you, along with your request for access, correction, or removal, send us a copy of your ID. To protect your privacy, Tech Mechancix B.V. asks you to black out your photo, MRZ (machine-readable zone, the strip with numbers at the bottom of the passport), document number, and citizen service number/social security number in this copy.
  4. Tech Mechancix B.V. will respond to your request as soon as possible, but at least within four weeks.

Right To Complain

  1. Internal handling
    1. If you have any complaints about the processing of (personal) data, Tech Mechancix B.V. would like to clear the air with you in mutual consultation. Please send your complaint to hr@TechMechancix.nl.
  2. Data Protection Authority
    1. Based on the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to submit a complaint to your own Data Protection Authority about our processing of your personal data. You can contact the lead supervisory authority of Tech Mechancix B.V., which is the Dutch DPA (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens). For more information on your right to complain, please visit the Dutch DPA's website: https://autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl/en.

Secure Connection Using SSL - Encryption

  1. SSL
    1. To protect you as a website visitor, Tech Mechancix B.V. uses secure encryption technologies. Using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) your personal data is encrypted (not visible to third parties) on our site. This way, you can be sure that your data will only be visible to you and our server.
  2. How can I identify the secure connection?
    1. We use SSL-encryption on all parts of our site where your data is used, such as when filling out the contact form. You can check and recognize this yourself. When you are on an encrypted part of our site, you will see a (green) lock symbol appearing in the lower-right or top-left corner of your browser. In addition, the URL of http:// changes to https:// which also indicates that the page is secure.
